全球前 500 大封裝測試大廠，服務據點遍及兩岸三地，在台灣有數座工廠，坐落於台中、彰化等地。其中多達數千台 Switch，使員工無法有效率的查找 Switch 資訊以及其下介接的設備。

在導入 PIXIS ATHENAC 之前，原為人工記錄 IP/MAC 對應表，需花費大量工時 之外，也無法即時更新。且任何有帳號密碼的人員皆可任意登入內網，導致工廠無法防範外來設備帶來的資安威脅。

自從導入 PIXIS ATHENAC 之後，進入內網的設備需要是授權的 MAC 才可以連入內網，有效達到外部設備的管理。

且以我們的系統來做 IP 派發，取代原先用 Excel 紀錄 IP / MAC 派發的資料，大 大提高了資料的準確性，還可以從儀表板上看到所有設備的上 / 下線即時資訊。

我們可以自動搜尋環境內所有 Switch 並匯出拓墣圖，且透過 SNMP 詢問其下 介接的設備以及設備的詳細資訊，若有任何設備出狀況，可於我們系統上快速找到該設備的位置，大幅減少處理事情所花費的時間。
The world's top 500 major packaging and testing factories, with service bases in three places, Taiwan, and Taiwan, are located in Taichung, Changhua and other places. There are as many as thousands of Switches, making it impossible for employees to efficiently find Switch information and the devices connected to it.

Before PIXIS ATHENAC was imported, the IP/MAC correspondence table was originally recorded manually, which required a lot of man-hours and could not be updated in real time. And anyone with an account and password can log in to the intranet at will, making the factory unable to prevent information security threats brought by external equipment.

Since the introduction of PIXIS ATHENAC, devices entering the intranet need to be authorized MACs before they can connect to the intranet, effectively achieving the management of external devices.

And we use our system to do IP distribution, instead of using Excel to record the IP/MAC distribution data, greatly improving the accuracy of the data, and you can also see the real-time information of all devices on/off line from the dashboard.

We can automatically search for all Switches in the environment and export the topology, and inquire about the connected devices and detailed information of the devices through SNMP. If any device is out of order, we can quickly find the location of the device on our system. Significantly reduce the time it takes to process things.

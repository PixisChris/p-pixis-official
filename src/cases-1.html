在台灣有六個廠區，數千台設備，但只有六位 IT，這六人需要做伺服器維護、 協助建置網路安全機制、落實資訊安全機制、網路安全系統規劃 / 管理、資安 事件追蹤管理、維護防火牆及電腦病毒防範軟體以及網路障礙排除。以上等等 繁雜且費時的工作，但工廠內員工時常自行攜帶手機或筆電等設備且未依規定 事先申請就連上內網，對公司內部資料造成極大的資安風險與管理上的困擾。

在導入 PIXIS ATHENAC 後，以網域群組標示 / 區隔所有 Windows 的設備，並 佐以 AD 帳號密碼驗證，以確定所有連上內網的設備皆是合法且員工使用的 MAC。

於偵測器上下線、伺服器服務重啟時，主動通知給各廠區管理者，以防服務中 斷或有任何閃失。

讓 IT 人員能將心力移至需要做長遠規劃的事項上。
A member of ASUS's subsidiaries, it currently has nearly 10,000 employees worldwide and has an annual turnover of more than 30 billion. It is an international manufacturer specializing in network communications and electronic product development.

There are six factories and thousands of equipment in Taiwan, but only six IT personnel. These six people need to do server maintenance, help build network security mechanisms, implement information security mechanisms, network security system planning/management, and information security Event tracking management, maintenance of firewalls and computer virus prevention software, and network obstacle removal. The above and so on are cumbersome and time-consuming tasks, but employees in the factory often bring their own mobile phones or laptops and other equipment and have not applied for connection to the intranet in advance according to regulations, which causes great information security risks and management problems for the company's internal data.

After importing PIXIS ATHENAC, mark/segregate all Windows devices with domain groups, and verify with AD account passwords to ensure that all devices connected to the intranet are legal and employees' MACs.

Block non-company equipment and self-owned equipment that employees have not applied for. Detect and display the device location and detailed information in real time, alert immediately when a problem occurs, and proactively notify the administrator, so that it can quickly obtain the location of the problem device and other detailed information.

When the detector goes online and offline, and the server service restarts, actively notify each plant manager to prevent service interruption or any error.

Allow IT staff to shift their minds to things that require long-term planning.
